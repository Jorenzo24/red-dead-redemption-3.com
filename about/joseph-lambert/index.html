Author

Joseph Lambert

Joseph Lambert is the writer and editor behind red-dead-redemption-3.com. He researches and writes every character profile and article on the site, from the Van der Linde gang to the long wait for Red Dead Redemption 3.

What I do here

I built this site to be the kind of Red Dead reference I always wanted: accurate, original and honest. That means writing each profile from the games rather than rewriting other wikis, verifying the specific details before publishing, and always being clear about the difference between what Rockstar has confirmed, what is only rumor, and what is my own speculation.

Why Red Dead

Few game worlds are written with the care Rockstar puts into Red Dead. Characters like Arthur Morgan and John Marston stand among the medium's best, and the universe rewards close attention. This site is my attempt to do that universe justice while we wait to see where the series goes next.

Editorial standards

Every piece I publish carries my byline and an "Updated" date, and follows the site's editorial policy. If you spot an error, I want to know, please get in touch.
