Red Dead Redemption 3 Release Date

What is the release date of Red Dead Redemption 3? Of course Rockstar has not revealed anything yet, nor even confirmed the game is in development. But we, the fans, are getting used to Rockstar's communication strategy, and we know all the same that the game really is in development, and that the release date announcement will come sooner or later.

May 23, 2022 By Joseph Lambert 1 min read

Release date estimate

Rockstar isn't exactly consistent when it comes to the time between two game releases: GTA Vice City in 2002, GTA San Andreas in 2004, GTA IV in 2009 and GTA V in 2013, while RDR2 came out eight years after the first entry.

So the optimistic scenario would be a release around 2025 only, and a realistic scenario around 2028. That's right, 10 years later, that is a long wait.

May patience be with you.
