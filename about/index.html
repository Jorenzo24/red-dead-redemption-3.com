About

About this site & how we cover Red Dead

red-dead-redemption-3.com is an independent fan encyclopedia and editorial hub for the Red Dead Redemption universe. We cover the characters and lore of RDR1 and RDR2, and we follow the long road toward a possible Red Dead Redemption 3, honestly and without the recycled hype that fills most of the web.

Our mission

Rockstar has not announced Red Dead Redemption 3. Analysts expect it years from now, after Grand Theft Auto VI. So our goal is not to chase clicks with fake "release date" stories, it is to build the most accurate, genuinely useful reference on the Red Dead universe, one page at a time, for the long haul.

How we cover RDR3: fact vs rumor vs speculation

This is the heart of our editorial policy. Whenever we write about the future of the series, we clearly separate three things:

  • Confirmed fact: something Rockstar or Take-Two has officially stated or shown.
  • Rumor: a claim from a source that is not official, flagged as such.
  • Speculation: our own informed analysis, always labelled as opinion.

We never present a rumor as a fact. If we cannot stand behind a detail, we do not publish it.

Original writing, not rewrites

Every character profile and article here is written from scratch. We do not paraphrase fan wikis or other encyclopedias, because derivative content caps the very authority and trust we are trying to build. Our character bios are our own work, checked against the games.

Accuracy & corrections

We verify specific details, dates, names, fates, who did what, before publishing, and we revise pages as needed. Each profile and article carries an author byline and an "Updated" date so you always know who wrote it and when it was last checked. Spotted a mistake? Tell us and we will fix it.

Images

Visuals are selected to illustrate the characters and the world. Rights are reviewed before publication. This is a fan project and makes no ownership claim over Rockstar's artwork or assets.

Who writes this

The site is written and edited by Joseph Lambert. You will find his byline on every profile and article.
