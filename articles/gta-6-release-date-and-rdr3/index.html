GTA 6 Finally Has a Date: What May 2026 Means for RDR3

On May 2, 2025, Rockstar finally pinned down Grand Theft Auto 6: May 26, 2026. It is the news the whole industry was waiting for, and indirectly, it is the most useful RDR3 timing signal we have had in years.

May 2, 2025 By Joseph Lambert 2 min read

The date, and the apology

Rockstar confirmed GTA 6 will launch on May 26, 2026, acknowledging it was later than the 2025 window many expected. After a single trailer in December 2023 and a long silence, a concrete release date is a major step.

Why this is really an RDR3 story

Rockstar does not build its two giant open worlds side by side. GTA 6 is the studio's entire focus, and historically the next Red Dead only enters full production once the current Grand Theft Auto has shipped and stabilized. So GTA 6's date is effectively the starting gun for any serious RDR3 work, not the finish line.

Doing the honest math

Here is where we stay disciplined about fact versus guess. Fact: GTA 6 is dated for May 2026. Fact: GTA 6 will then get years of online support. Reasonable inference: RDR3's development ramps up after that, which points to a release toward the end of the decade or beyond. Pure speculation: any specific RDR3 year. Anyone giving you one today is guessing, and now you can see exactly why.
