Red Dead Redemption timeline

The story of the Van der Linde gang across the Red Dead Redemption saga, in order, from the gang's rise to its long, tragic fall. Heads up: this page contains major spoilers for both games.

  1. c. 1877

    Dutch and Hosea take in Arthur

    Dutch van der Linde and Hosea Matthews, who founded the gang together, take in an orphaned teenage Arthur Morgan and raise him as a gunman and a son.

  2. c. 1888

    John Marston joins the gang

    Dutch takes in another orphan, a young John Marston, who grows up alongside Arthur as the gang's next generation.

  3. 1895

    Jack Marston is born

    John and Abigail Roberts have a son, Jack. John briefly abandons the family, straining his bond with Arthur for years.

  4. 1899

    The Blackwater disaster

    A robbery in Blackwater goes catastrophically wrong. The gang flees east into the frozen mountains of Ambarino with money it cannot spend, the opening of Red Dead Redemption 2.

  5. 1899

    Sadie joins; the gang regroups

    The O'Driscolls murder the husband of Sadie Adler, and the gang takes her in. As they regroup, they rescue the young Irishman Sean MacGuire, only for him to be gunned down soon after at Rhodes.

  6. 1899

    The Saint Denis bank robbery

    A bank job in Saint Denis collapses into chaos. Hosea Matthews and the young gunman Lenny Summers are killed, the gang's conscience and its future lost in a single day.

  7. 1899

    Micah's betrayal and Arthur's end

    Micah Bell is revealed as a Pinkerton informant who rotted the gang from within. Arthur Morgan dies confronting the truth, buying John and his family their escape.

  8. 1907

    Beecher's Hope and the hunt for Micah

    John Marston builds an honest life at a ranch. With Sadie Adler and Charles Smith, he hunts Micah down at Mount Hagen, where an aged Dutch reappears one last time.

  9. 1911

    John's reckoning

    Coerced by government agents, John hunts his former brothers, including Bill Williamson and Javier Escuella, and finally Dutch, who steps off a cliff rather than be taken. This is the spine of the original Red Dead Redemption.

  10. 1911

    The death of John Marston

    With the work done, the government turns on him. John gets his family to safety and is gunned down at Beecher's Hope, one of gaming's most famous deaths.

  11. 1914

    Jack's revenge

    After his mother Abigail dies, a grown Jack Marston tracks down Edgar Ross, the agent who destroyed his family, and kills him, closing the saga's long cycle of revenge.
